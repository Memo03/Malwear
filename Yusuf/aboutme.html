About us


For our clothing company, Malwear, we focus on and vouch for diversity in the tech industry. Throughout the years, gentrification has been a huge issue and topic. Gentrification has affected many communities and has resulted in a large divide between social classes and ethnicities. The tech industry is a huge factor in the rise of gentrification. The technology industry has been on the rise and continues to grow with no sign of stopping. However, many tech companies are dominated by one race and they lack diversity. A solution to this problem is offering coding classes and programs. One example of these coding classes is Mission Bit. Mission bit is an organization that focuses on teaching the youth how to program. Mission Bit teaches a wide range of unexperienced teenagers from all types of income and ethnicities.
